About Smart Home Gear Reviews

Honest smart home reviews from real homes, not labs.

Our Approach

We test smart home devices in actual living spaces with real families. Because a device that works great in a demo doesn't always work in a house with thick walls, multiple ecosystems, and kids who mess with settings.

What We Cover

Smart speakers, displays, security cameras, door locks, thermostats, lighting, sensors, and everything that connects your home.

What You'll Learn

Real setup difficulty (not marketing claims), ecosystem compatibility, app quality, reliability over time, and whether it's worth your money.

Our Promise

We test across Alexa, Google Home, and HomeKit. We tell you about the frustrating parts, not just the features. We help you build a smart home that actually works.
