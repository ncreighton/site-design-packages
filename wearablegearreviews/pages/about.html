About Wearable Gear Reviews

In-depth wearable tech reviews from weeks of real-world use.

Why Trust Our Reviews?

We actually wear everything we review. For weeks. Not hours. Because day-1 impressions don't tell you about battery life at day 14 or how the band feels after a month of daily use.

What We Cover

Smartwatches (Apple, Samsung, Garmin, Fitbit), fitness trackers, smart rings, health monitors, and wireless audio wearables.

What Makes Us Different

We test health tracking accuracy against medical-grade devices. We measure real battery life, not marketing claims. We compare value across price points.

Our Standards

No pay-for-play reviews. No perfect scores for flawed products. Honest assessments you can actually use to make buying decisions.
