About Sprout and Spruce

Gardening wisdom from real seasons of growth (and failure).

Our Approach

We believe gardening should be joyful, not stressful. We share what works, what doesn't, and why sometimes plants just die (and that's okay).

What We Cover

Vegetables, flowers, indoor plants, garden design, organic methods, and troubleshooting. From windowsill herbs to backyard vegetable gardens.

Who This Is For

Beginners who want to start growing. Experienced gardeners looking for new ideas. Anyone who's killed a plant and wondered what went wrong.

Our Promise

Patient guidance. Honest about challenges. Celebrating small wins. Regional awareness. No judgment if your tomatoes failed—ours have too.
