About Pulse Gear Reviews

Fitness tech reviews from athletes, for athletes.

Real Testing, Real Results

We don't just unbox gear and read specs. Every product gets tested through actual workouts—runs, lifts, training sessions. Because that's the only way to know if something actually works.

What We Cover

Running watches, fitness trackers, heart rate monitors, smart scales, wireless earbuds, and everything that helps you train smarter.

Our Testing Process

Minimum 2-week testing period. Real workout data. Comparison against competitors. Honest pros and cons. No perfect scores for imperfect products.

Who We Are

Athletes and fitness enthusiasts who geek out about performance data. We've tested hundreds of products so you can find the right one faster.
